Welcome! 
I am currently a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Tartu, with [Danel Ahman](https://danel.ahman.ee/) as my supervisor. 
At the moment, my work revolves around type systems for programming languages with focus on time-sensitive resources, and my work relies mostly on the [Agda](https://agda.readthedocs.io/en/latest/getting-started/what-is-agda.html) proof assistant. 

Previously, I was a PhD student in Computer Science at [IRIF](https://www.irif.fr/en/index), in Paris, France.
[Delia Kesner](https://www.irif.fr/~kesner/) and [Pablo Barenbaum](https://foones.github.io/) were my supervisors. My PhD was funded by the [MathInParis2020](https://sciencesmaths-paris.fr/nos-programmes/master?view=article&id=132) fellowship of [FSMP](https://sciencesmaths-paris.fr/) cofunded by [MSCA](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/).

My research interests include the λ-calculus and logic, type theory, and semantics of functional and concurrent programming languages.
